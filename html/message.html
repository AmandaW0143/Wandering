"Time doesn't pass here as it does elsewhere."

The words send a chill through you. What does it mean? And why was it left here? The air around you thickens, as though the forest itself is holding its breath. The lantern in your hand begins to grow warm, almost too warm. The flame steadies for a brief moment before sputtering again. Its light suddenly becomes unnaturally bright, casting a long shadow across the bamboo stalks.
